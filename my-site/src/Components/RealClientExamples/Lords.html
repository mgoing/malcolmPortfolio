Our Story

Built on Hard Work and Good Advice

Lords Hardware started as a small family operation and grew into the community's go-to for home improvement. We've always believed that the right product, paired with honest guidance, makes every job easier.

Storefront photo
Who We Are

Lords Hardware has been a fixture in this community for over 30 years. What started as a small storefront has grown into a full-service hardware and home improvement destination — without losing the personal touch that sets us apart from the big box stores.

We know most of our customers by name. When you walk in with a leaky pipe or a deck to build, our staff will walk you through exactly what you need — no overselling, no guessing. Just straight answers from people who know the trade.

Our shelves are stocked with everything from everyday fasteners and hand tools to paint, plumbing fittings, electrical supplies, and seasonal items. We take pride in carrying quality brands and keeping popular items reliably in stock.

Lords is locally owned and operated. Every purchase you make here supports our staff, our building, and our community — not a corporate headquarters somewhere else.
